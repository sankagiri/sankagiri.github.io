## Join Our Team

We are always looking for highly motivated students interested in pursuing research in data science and AI. 

### For Prospective Students

If you are interested in joining our research group, please:

1. **Ph.D. Applicants**: Apply through the IIT Madras Ph.D. admissions process. You may also send me an email with your CV and research interests.

2. **M.Tech Students**: Current IIT Madras M.Tech students interested in doing their thesis with us should send an email with their background and interests.

3. **B.Tech Students**: Undergraduate students at IIT Madras interested in research projects or internships can reach out via email.

4. **Visiting Students/Interns**: We occasionally host visiting students and interns. Please send your CV, transcripts, and a brief statement of interest.

### What We Look For

- Strong mathematical and programming background
- Genuine interest in research
- Good communication skills
- Ability to work both independently and collaboratively
- Prior experience in machine learning/AI is a plus but not required

Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions about joining our team!
