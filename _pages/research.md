## Research Overview

My research focuses on developing novel machine learning and artificial intelligence techniques to address challenging problems in data science. I lead a research group at IIT Madras that works on both theoretical foundations and practical applications of AI.

## Research Areas

### Machine Learning and Deep Learning
We develop new algorithms and architectures for learning from complex, high-dimensional data. Our work spans supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning paradigms.

### Natural Language Processing
We work on understanding and generating natural language, including multilingual NLP, low-resource languages, and domain-specific language models.

### Computer Vision
Our research in computer vision includes object detection, image segmentation, video analysis, and visual understanding for real-world applications.

### Data Analytics and Big Data
We develop scalable methods for analyzing large-scale datasets, including distributed computing frameworks and efficient data processing techniques.

### AI for Social Good
We apply AI techniques to solve problems with social impact, including healthcare, education, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.
