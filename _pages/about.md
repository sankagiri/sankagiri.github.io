Welcome to my academic webpage! I am a Professor in the Department of Data Science and AI at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras), one of India's premier institutions for higher education and research.

## About Me

My research interests span across multiple areas of data science and artificial intelligence, with a focus on developing innovative solutions to real-world problems. I lead a vibrant research group working on cutting-edge projects that advance the frontiers of knowledge in our field.

## Research Interests

My work focuses on:
- Machine Learning and Deep Learning
- Natural Language Processing
- Computer Vision
- Data Analytics and Big Data
- AI for Social Good

## Academic Background

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and have been actively involved in research, teaching, and mentoring students at various levels. My work has been published in top-tier conferences and journals, and I actively collaborate with researchers worldwide.

## Teaching

I am passionate about education and regularly teach courses at both undergraduate and graduate levels, covering topics in data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and related areas. I believe in fostering a strong learning environment that encourages critical thinking and innovation.

For more information about my research, publications, teaching activities, and research team, please explore the different sections of this website.
