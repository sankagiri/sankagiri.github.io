I will be joining the [Wadhwani School of Data Science and AI](https://wsai.iitm.ac.in) at [IIT Madras](https://www.iitm.ac.in) as an Assistant Professor, starting April 2026. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at EPFL, Switzerland, working with Prof. [Matthias Grossglauser] (https://people.epfl.ch/matthias.grossglauser). My research focuses on the designing models and algorithms for learning human preferences from comparison data, with applications in recommendation systems and search. I am also interested in other domains where learning preferences is important, such as LLM alignment.

I graduated from [IIT Bombay]((https://www.iitb.ac.in)) with a B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering in 2016. I obtained my MS & PhD from the [Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://ece.illinois.edu) department at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. I was advised by Prof. [Bruce Hajek](https://hajek.ece.illinois.edu) and I was affiliated with the [Coordinated Science Lab](https://csl.illinois.edu/). During my PhD, I worked on proving security and performance guarantees for blockchain protocols. For my Masters thesis, I developed a new algorithm for community detection in preferential attachment graphs. 

Broadly, I am interested in modeling human-in-the-loop systems, where humans and algorithms interact in a decentralised fashion, each subject to their own constraints and limited information. I am particularly interested in drawing new practical insights into these systems via a deep theoretical understanding of these systems. I use a variety of tools from probability, statistics, optimisation, and network science in my research.
